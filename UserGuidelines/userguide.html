User Guidelines

Welcome to Brainwave Digital Library. Please adhere to the following rules and guidelines to ensure a smooth experience for everyone.

  • Maintain respectful behavior towards other users.
  • Do not share your account credentials with others.
  • Ensure all borrowed digital materials are returned on time.

  • Content is for personal use only and cannot be redistributed.
  • Respect copyright laws and intellectual property rights.
  • Report any inappropriate or harmful content to administrators.

  • Use a strong, unique password for your account.
  • Update your password regularly for security.
  • Immediately report suspicious activity to the support team.
